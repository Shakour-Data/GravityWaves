1. Management Documentation

These documents are used for planning, directing, and monitoring the project:

  • Project Plan: Includes goals, scope, schedule, budget, and human resources.
  • Business Model: Market analysis, target users, and revenue strategy.
  • Risk Management Plan: Risk analysis and mitigation strategies.
  • Requirement Specification: Detailed functional and non-functional requirements.
  • Legal & Compliance Documents: Contracts, licenses, and legal standards.
  • Release & Deployment Strategy: Version release plans, deployment, and support.
